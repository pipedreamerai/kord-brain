# [[pit-312]] — 2nd Pass HP Pump Discharge Transmitter

IFM Efector PN2292 pressure transmitter. Range: 0–1450 psi, 4-20 mA, LED display, 24VDC. Monitors 2nd-pass Grundfos CRNE pump discharge pressure.

## Function

High-pressure measurement at the 2nd-pass pump discharge. Protects 2nd-pass membranes (ESPA2-MAX low-pressure series) from over-pressure. 2nd-pass operating pressures are typically lower than 1st-pass. Listed as a shutdown alarm: "High pump discharge pressure (2nd pass)."

## Interlocks

Listed shutdown in [[bid_firm_quote]]: "High pump discharge pressure (2nd pass, 2nd Pass)."
