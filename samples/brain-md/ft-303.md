# [[ft-303]] — 2nd Pass Product Flow Transmitter

Georg Fischer Signet 3-2537-6C-P0 paddlewheel flow transmitter. Range: 0.3–20 ft/s, 4-20 mA output. Monitors final RODI product water flow to the buffer tank.

## Function

Measures the 2nd-pass RO permeate that enters the RODI buffer tank. Design target: 100 GPM. The PLC uses [[ft-303]] for:
- Overall recovery display on HMI (alongside [[ft-301]])
- Call-for-water signal coordination with the electrolyzer
- Low-flow shutdown (if product flow drops below threshold)
