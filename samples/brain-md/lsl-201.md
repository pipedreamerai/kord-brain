# [[lsl-201]] — Pump Suction Level Switch #1

Grundfos rigid suction lance level alarm, part 98071114. Digital switch output. Mounted at the minimum allowable liquid level in the pump suction vessel/header. LSL-202 and LSL-203 provide the same function on subsequent pump stages.

## Function

Primary dry-run protection for the RO feed pump. If liquid level at the pump suction falls below the switch setpoint, [[lsl-201]] opens its digital contact. The Siemens S7-1215C PLC immediately initiates a shutdown to prevent pump cavitation and seal damage.

## Standby interlock

The RO Unit Description ([[bid_firm_quote]]) documents: "Dry contact inputs are provided for... pump suction and pump discharge pressure switches." [[lsl-201]] is one of those dry contact inputs. When [[lsl-201]] trips, the unit enters standby and flushes periodically.
