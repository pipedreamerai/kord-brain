# [[hv-507]] — Buffer Tank Outlet Butterfly Valve

Bray Controls / Bray International lug-style lever-operated butterfly valve. Model 31-0400-11010-324LVR. 4" body, 175 PSI DI EPDM seat. Manual lever actuator.

## Function

Primary isolation valve on the RODI buffer tank outlet supply to the electrolyzer feed header. Normally open during operation. Closed for tank isolation during maintenance or system shutdown.

## Operational context

Product water from the RO 2nd pass fills the buffer tank. [[lit-501]] monitors the tank level. [[hv-507]] is on the supply line from the tank to the electrolyzer. The N2 blanket (PCV-500) and conservation vent (CBV-501) manage tank pressure while [[hv-507]] controls outlet flow isolation.
