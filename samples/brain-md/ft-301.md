# [[ft-301]] — 1st Pass Feed Flow Transmitter

Georg Fischer Signet 3-2537-6C-P0 paddlewheel flow transmitter. Range: 0.3–20 ft/s, 4-20 mA output. Monitors 1st-pass RO feed water flow rate.

## Function

Measures volumetric flow at the 1st-pass membrane array inlet. Target: ~128 GPM (44-membrane, 100 GPM product unit). Used by the Siemens S7-1215C PLC for:
- Recovery rate calculation
- Low-feed-flow shutdown alarm

## Interlocks

If [[ft-301]] reads below the low-flow setpoint, PLC triggers a shutdown alarm and stops the 1st-pass pump. This protects membranes from operating outside design flux range.
