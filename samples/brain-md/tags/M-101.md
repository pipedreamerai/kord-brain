# [[M-101]] — Feedwater Pump Motor

100 HP, 480V, 3-phase, 60 Hz, TEFC induction motor. Service: drives feedwater pump [[P-101]].

## Power

Fed from [[MCC-1]] through breaker [[CB-101]]. Across-the-line starter.

## Interlocks

Will not start unless [[LSL-201]] and [[CV-301]] interlock contacts are satisfied at [[IR-2]].
