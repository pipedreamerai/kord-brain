# [[P-101]] — Feedwater Pump

50 GPM, 100 PSI centrifugal feedwater pump. Driven by motor [[M-101]].

## Process

Draws from [[T-101]] and discharges through [[CV-301]] into the electrolyzer stack inlet header. Supplies DI water at 5 bar.

## Protection

[[LSL-201]] on [[T-101]] trips the motor to prevent dry running.
