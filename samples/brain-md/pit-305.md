# [[pit-305]] — 1st Pass HP Pump Discharge Transmitter

IFM Efector PN2292 pressure transmitter. Range: 0–1450 psi, 4-20 mA, LED display, 24VDC. Monitors 1st-pass Grundfos CRNE32-8 pump discharge pressure.

## Function

High-pressure measurement at the 1st-pass pump discharge. If pressure exceeds the configured shutdown setpoint, the PLC triggers a high-pump-discharge-pressure alarm and stops the pump to prevent membrane damage. Typical operating pressure: 150–300 psi depending on feed water quality and membrane condition.

## Interlocks

[[pit-305]] is a listed shutdown alarm in [[ro_spec]]: "High pump discharge pressure (1st pass)."
