# [[lit-501]] — RODI Buffer Tank Level Transmitter

Rosemount 3051L2AA2AD11AAM5Q4 differential pressure level transmitter. Range: ±250 inH₂O, 4-20 mA output, 3" flange connection. Monitors level in the RODI buffer tank (also referred to as LT-501 in the Loose Parts List).

## Function

Provides continuous 4-20 mA level signal to the Siemens S7-1215C PLC. Two control actions:
1. **High level → Standby:** when [[lit-501]] indicates tank full, PLC puts RO into standby mode and initiates periodic permeate flush.
2. **Low level → Resume:** when level drops (electrolyzer drawing water through [[hv-507]]), PLC restarts RO.

## Procurement note

[[lit-501]] is the **longest-lead instrument** at 14 weeks. This is the Rosemount 3051L series with 3" flange — not a stock item. Late order risks electrolyzer commissioning delay.
